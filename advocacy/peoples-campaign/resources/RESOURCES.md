# RESOURCES: MENTAL HEALTH, LEGAL AID, AND COMMUNITY SUPPORT

This page provides resources for individuals and communities affected by the current crisis. If you or someone you know is struggling, help is available.

---

## MENTAL HEALTH RESOURCES

### Crisis Lines

| Resource | Contact | Description |
|:---------|:--------|:------------|
| **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** | Call or text **988** | Free, 24/7 support for anyone in emotional distress or suicidal crisis. |
| **Veterans Crisis Line** | Call **988**, then press **1** | Specialized support for veterans, service members, and their families. |
| **Crisis Text Line** | Text **HOME** to **741741** | Free, 24/7 text-based crisis support. |
| **SAMHSA National Helpline** | 1-800-662-4357 | Free, 24/7 treatment referral and information service for mental health and substance use disorders. |
